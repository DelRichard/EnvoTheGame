## Game Description

EnvoTheGame is a 2.5D, story-driven experience where the protagonist, Envo, sets out to rebuild their village.

After toxic waste from Neon City devastates the land, the once-thriving home becomes overrun by mutated vegetable monsters. By assisting a eccentric village scientist with various tasks, Envo gradually restores the community, bringing people back together and improving the village’s quality of life.
